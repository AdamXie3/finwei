The 50/30/20 Rule

50% - Needs

  • Rent/Housing
  • Utilities
  • Groceries
  • Transportation
  • Insurance

30% - Wants

  • Entertainment
  • Dining out
  • Shopping
  • Hobbies
  • Travel

20% - Savings

  • Emergency fund
  • Retirement
  • Investments
  • Debt repayment

UAE-Specific Budgeting Tips

Housing Costs in UAE

Consider annual rent payments (1-4 cheques) and housing fees (5% of rent)

Transportation

Factor in car payments, Salik, parking, or public transport (Nol card)

Utilities

DEWA bills, internet, phone plans, and district cooling if applicable

Groceries

Compare prices between hypermarkets and local markets
