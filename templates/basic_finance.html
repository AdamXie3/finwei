Basic Financial Concepts

Key Financial Terms

Assets

Things you own that have value (cash, investments, property)

Liabilities

Money you owe (loans, credit card debt, mortgages)

Net Worth

The difference between your assets and liabilities

Interest Rate

The cost of borrowing money or the return on investments

UAE Banking System

  • Central Bank of the UAE (CBUAE) - The regulatory authority
  • Types of banks: Commercial, Islamic, Investment
  • Common account types: Current, Savings, Fixed Deposit
  • Digital banking and mobile payments
